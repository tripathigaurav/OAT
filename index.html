📋 Auto-Mark Setup

To enable automatic attendance when you connect to office WiFi, pick your OS:

  1. Download auto-attendance.sh & LaunchAgent plist
  2. Put both files in a folder (e.g. ~/Desktop/OAT/)
  3. Open Terminal and run: chmod +x ~/Desktop/OAT/auto-attendance.sh
  4. Install the LaunchAgent: cp ~/Desktop/OAT/com.oat.wifiattendance.plist ~/Library/LaunchAgents/ launchctl load ~/Library/LaunchAgents/com.oat.wifiattendance.plist
  5. Done! ✨ Runs automatically on WiFi change & login.

To stop: launchctl unload ~/Library/LaunchAgents/com.oat.wifiattendance.plist

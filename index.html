📋 Auto-Mark Setup

⚡ One-line reinstall command — copy & run in terminal:

To enable automatic attendance when you connect to office WiFi, pick your OS:

Open Terminal and paste:

curl -sL https://tripathigaurav.github.io/OAT/install-mac.command | bash
How to stop auto-tracking (Mac)

launchctl unload ~/Library/LaunchAgents/com.oat.wifiattendance.plist

Open PowerShell (Win+R → type powershell → Enter) and paste:

powershell -ExecutionPolicy Bypass -Command "irm https://tripathigaurav.github.io/OAT/install-win.ps1 | iex"
How to stop auto-tracking (Windows)

Unregister-ScheduledTask -TaskName "OAT-WiFiAttendance" -Confirm:$false
